Welcome to Hilo, the lush and vibrant city found on the Big island of Hawaii. Nestled on the east side of the island, Hilo is an amazing place to visit to see its wonderful landscapes and jaw-dropping waterfalls. Discover the beauty of the Big Island in a town that captures the essence of Hawaii's natural diversity.

This city, along with several of the other cities within Hawaii, have a complex history regarding ownership due to the annexation to the United States as a US territory in 1898 and its approval to statehood in 1959. According to Wikipedia, the first human inhabitants of Hilo arrived in 1100 AD. This time was known as "Ancient Hawaii." The city was already established by the time the US annexed Hawaii, so there is no official incorporation date for this city.

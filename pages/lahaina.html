Lahaina

Welcome to Lahaina, a coastal town located on the island of Maui. Filled with bustling hisoric districts, vibrant art galleries, and informative museums; there are a plethora of things to enjoy. Whether its strolling through the streets or embarking on ocean adventures, this coastal gem promises an unforgettable escape.

This city, along with several of the other cities within Hawaii, have a complex history regarding owndership due to the annexation to the annexation to the United States as a US territory in 1898 and its approval to statehood in 1959. According to Lahainatown.com , Lahaina had been inhabited for hundreds of years and was previously the capital of Hawaii in 1802. There were no resources online that I could find as a definitive incorporation date due to undocumented history and colonization.

Honolulu

Welcome to Honolulu, a tropical paradise that seamlessly blends the allure of urban sophistication with the tranquility of island living. Located on the island of Oahu. Honolulu stands as the vibrant capital city that captivates visitors with breathtaking landscapes, cultural richness, and warm hospitality.

Honolulu, along with several of the other cities within Hawaii, have a complex history regarding ownership due to the annexation to the United States as a US territory in 1898 and its approval to statehood in 1959. According to Wikipedia, Honolulu was incorporated as a city in 1907. Due to a higher cost of living in an urban city, the average household annual income ($92,600 per year) is higher in Honolulu compared to other cities in Hawaii.
